The Kid Version of Me

A Happy and Cute Little Kid


16 October 2004, 09:45 AM. Yes, that is when I was born. When it all began. When I came to this "wonderful" world. Anyway, Don't you think it is insane that all of us humans get a chance to be born? it's roughly 1 in 400 trillion chance a human like us could possibly born and live to this world. Have you ever asked yourself – what are the chances of you being born as the person you are, in that shape and form? Yeah, a bit insane, right?

There's one time that I really think about it. What if I never came to this world? What if I was born as an animal? What if...? Is there any reason why? What is the point? What?... And then I just forget about it, trying to live my life to the fullest, and pursue all of my dreams. I think we all should probably do that :D

Growing as a kid. I was pretty happy and (probably) smart as a kid. I'm very very grateful that I have a wonderful and loving parents, even though I wasn't born in a rich family, or a big house, elite school, and all. But, I think I had a very memorable childhood. For example, that one time I fell in the bathroom and my nose hit the edge of something (I can't remember). And... yeah, my nose immediately bleeds and the wound is quite wide. My upper nose in between the eyes had to be stitched up and I really remembered how it went through. I remembered that I cried for so long while I was taken to the hospital. On the way I was just probably blacked out because I'm tired from crying and woke up on the day after. Pretty memorable, don't you think?

So, the fell incident happened in 2009, I think a few days after the picture of "little me" above was taken. And now, I still have the stitches on my nose. Everytime that I noticed that stiches in the mirror, I just smile remembering my childhood. On 2009-2010, finally I went to a kindergarten near my house. Back then, I think I was just 4 years old who was bored and just following my older friends to entering kindergarten. That's pretty much why I'm only attending kindergarten for 1 year, and forcing my parents to sign me up on the same Elementary School as my older friends. I have many friends, but Felix and Sheila are my closest friend. We used to play together everyday, everytime. I had so many memories with them that causing me to forgot them right now.

Felix and I have been bestfriend for 13 years now, but I don't know where Sheila is after I moved out to a new house. I'm curious about where she is now, but all my old friend didnt know :)

Me, Myself, and I. In this teenager years, as a common teenager all around the world, I have a problem getting to know about myself. How to recognize yourself, your thoughts, your strength, your weakness and your emotions? I'm kinda struggling with that. But thankfully, as I'm entering age 15-16, I'm slowly getting to know things about myself (to be honest, the pandemic and quarantine things is one thing that helped me get to know myself). As you can see on the images above, I am very calm, kinda smart and clever, ambitious yet very lazy, flexible, careless, and easily get bored. I recognize that is really describing about me, as all of my closest friend telling me the same thing when I asked them about myself.

One thing that helped me a little knowing about myself is the "Myers-Briggs Type Indicator". The test was telling me that I'm either INTP (Introverted-Intuition-Thinking-Perceiving) or INTJ (Introverted-Intuition-Thinking-Judging). I think that the test was pretty close describing about me, although I know that many experts are explaining about the weakness of the test. For those three things (Introverted-Intuition-Thinking), i really agreed about it, as I'm an Introvert and enjoying a time alone, and I kinda don't like to be around people. And then the Intuition-Thinking describes me well, as my friend was telling me that I'm the "logical and rational" person. About Perceiving and Judging, I don't really know because the test indicates that I'm more or less 50:50 Perceiving-Judging.

I don't know what else to write, but for now I'm trying to get to know myself about my strength and weakness. I think that those are really important to improving myself, being a better person, and using them to reach my goals and dreams.

Anyway, just as I told you before, I learned SO MUCH in this school. Like, I learned about Chemistry (of course), Social Stuff, Time Management Stuff, Self-Control thingies, and probably just how things works. And I think, I decided to learn here in this school is one of the things that will change my life in the future.

SOC EKSAKTA 2021
How did it went
The Event
SOC EKSAKTA 2021. It is one of our program in "Bidang Pendidikan". The preparation for the event was only 3 months and there's so things that challenging for us in the preparation, but thankfully it did went well (although there's many misscommunication). Our teacher, Mr Wahyu said that the event that we hosted, is equal to a University-Level event. When I heard that, I doubted it for some reasons, but it is not as bad as I think. Everything went well despite it is our 1st big program after a year inactive and has no event, but there's so many miscommunication on D-Day. To be honest, I struggled a bit handling the event because the chief commitee (Ketua Pelaksana) was involved in a traffic accident a week before the event started.

On SOC EKSAKTA 2021 commitee, I'm the Main Secretary, handling the administration, letter, registration, proposal, report, Etc. For me, it was challenging because it is my first time as a secretary, and to be fair I think I did well on my first time although im lacking in "precisely & thoroughly" things. I learned a lot from that event, and I think it is improving my softskills, like communication, administration, and public speaking. And yes, the event wouldn't be successful without all the amazing people (told you they are good :D) in the commitee, shout out to them!

All online-learning thingies
Online Meeting Allday
Tired
Online learning/Online school. The thing that we were so happy and excited about at the first 2 weeks, and then went "What is this" on the weeks after. Talking about online learning, there's so much aspects and factors to talk about. But, I'll just write my opinion and experience on this online learning that i feel like a 2-eyed-sword for all of us.

Personally, I don't like online learning. It is really keeping me in my comfort zone, why? You can wake up at 8 AM everyday, watching movies/TV series all day long, or just having fun and ignoring all the lessons or tasks given. Besides that, I'm struggling with the facility, I feel like that I'm limited to do what I want to do because I didn't have the equipment. One other reason, I wasted so much time on this online learning things and didn't maximize all my potential. I felt the difference between the 6 months online-learning and the 4 weeks when I did go to school, practice in the lab, and really do the things that I'm supposed to do. I do really think that I learned more in the 4 weeks learning offline in school than learning 6 months online.

But, I try to look at the positive things. For example, I didn't have to went through a 20 minutes - 12 Kilometers transport going to school everyday, and I had more time to actually connect with myself and my family, and more time to do what I wanted to do. I just hope on this remaining online learning, I can maximize all my self potential, and keep growing and improving despite all the negative things that is happening around me. I do understand that every student faces a different struggles, and I just hope the best for all of us.
